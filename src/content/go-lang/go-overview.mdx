<SectionSubheading id="toc-philosophy">Why Go is opinionated (and that's the point)</SectionSubheading>

Coming from JavaScript/TypeScript, Go will feel restrictive at first. That's by design. Fewer choices means more consistency across every Go codebase you encounter.
