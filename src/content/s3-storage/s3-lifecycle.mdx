<SectionIntro>
You don't have to manually move objects between classes. S3 Lifecycle Rules let you automate transitions and deletions based on age. This is where the real cost savings happen.
</SectionIntro>

<Explainer title="Think of it like email rules">
Just like an email rule that says "move messages older than 30 days to Archive," a lifecycle rule says "move objects older than 30 days to Standard-IA, and after 90 days move them to Glacier."
</Explainer>
