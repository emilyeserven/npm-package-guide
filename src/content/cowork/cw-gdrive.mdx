<SectionSubheading id="toc-limitations">Connector limitations</SectionSubheading>

<Gotcha>
The Google Drive connector lets Cowork **search and read** files, but it cannot create, rename, move, or delete files in Drive directly. For write operations, Cowork can prepare files locally which you then upload, or use the Chrome extension for browser-based Drive actions.
</Gotcha>
