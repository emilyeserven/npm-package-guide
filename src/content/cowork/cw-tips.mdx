<SectionSubheading id="toc-prompts">Writing effective prompts</SectionSubheading>

<DefinitionTable termHeader="Tip" descHeader="Example">
  <DefRow term="Be specific about structure">Use "sort into folders named A, B, C" instead of "organize this".</DefRow>
  <DefRow term="Specify what NOT to do">Say "don't delete any files" or "skip hidden files starting with .".</DefRow>
  <DefRow term="Ask for a dry run first">Say "show me what you'd do before making changes" to preview the plan.</DefRow>
  <DefRow term="Set naming patterns explicitly">Provide a concrete pattern like "YYYY-MM-DD_name.ext" rather than "use consistent names".</DefRow>
  <DefRow term="Use global instructions">Set folder or global instructions so Cowork always follows your conventions without re-stating them.</DefRow>
</DefinitionTable>

<SectionSubheading id="toc-sessions">Session management</SectionSubheading>

<Gotcha>
Claude Desktop must remain open for the entire task. Closing the app or putting your computer to sleep ends the session.
</Gotcha>
