<SectionSubheading id="toc-infra">{'\u2601\uFE0F'} Infrastructure reality</SectionSubheading>

<SectionList>
<ColItem>Kubernetes manages **WHERE** your containers run, **HOW MANY** copies, **WHEN** to restart them, and **HOW** traffic reaches them. It's a container orchestration platform.</ColItem>
