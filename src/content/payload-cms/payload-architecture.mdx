<SectionSubheading id="toc-database">Database adapters</SectionSubheading>

Payload uses a **database adapter pattern**. You choose your database â€” PostgreSQL (via Drizzle ORM), MongoDB, or SQLite â€” and Payload handles the rest. The adapter is swappable, so you can change databases without rewriting your schema.

<Gotcha>
Payload auto-manages migrations for PostgreSQL. When you change your collection schemas, Payload generates migration files that you can review and apply â€” similar to how Rails or Django handle schema changes.
</Gotcha>

  <DefRow term="SQLite" definition="File-based. Great for local development, edge deployments (Cloudflare D1), or small projects." />
