<SectionSubheading id="toc-checklist">Quick checklist</SectionSubheading>

<SectionList>
<ColItem>Use <code>npm ci</code>, not <code>npm install</code>, in every CI job.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Use a YAML linter in your editor to catch indentation errors before pushing.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Never hardcode secrets &mdash; use the repository's encrypted secrets store.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Start with a single, simple workflow. Add complexity only when needed.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Stick to <code>ubuntu-latest</code> runners to conserve free-tier minutes.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Check the Actions tab logs when something fails &mdash; the error messages are usually clear.</ColItem>
</SectionList>
