<SectionSubheading id="toc-takeaways">Key takeaways</SectionSubheading>

<SectionList>
<ColItem>Every workflow starts with <code>name:</code> and <code>on:</code> (the trigger).</ColItem>
<ColItem>The <code>jobs:</code> block contains one or more jobs. Each job gets its own fresh runner.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Almost every job starts with <code>actions/checkout@v4</code> to clone the repo.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Use <code>run:</code> for shell commands and <code>uses:</code> for pre-built Actions.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Use <code>npm ci</code> (not <code>npm install</code>) in CI &mdash; it's faster and stricter.</ColItem>
<ColItem>Caching (<code>cache: 'npm'</code>) dramatically speeds up repeated runs.</ColItem>
</SectionList>
