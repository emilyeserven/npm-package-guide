<SectionList>
<ColItem>**Start with branch protection** &mdash; requiring CI to pass before merging is the single most impactful thing you can do.</ColItem>
<ColItem>**Add caching next** &mdash; it's one line of config and saves minutes on every run.</ColItem>
<ColItem>**Add matrix builds** when you need to support multiple Node versions or operating systems.</ColItem>
<ColItem>**Add secrets** when you need to deploy or call external APIs from your pipeline.</ColItem>
